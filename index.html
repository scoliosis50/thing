Me and space

Hi, mmy name is, and I love space. As a kid, I was very curious about the world around me. We lived in a relatively dark-lit area, so every night I looked up at the stars and the moon from my bedroom window. Once I got access to the internet, I soon learned more about them and continued to research, leading me down rabbit holes of information and complex math. Though I didn't understand much of it at the time, I was keen on learning more, and that still continues today.
